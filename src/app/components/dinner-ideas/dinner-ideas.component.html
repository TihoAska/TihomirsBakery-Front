dinner ideas.

finish your day with a light meal that won't cause you problems going to sleep.

BOILED EGGS

A light meal full of protein and healthy fats. You can never go wrong with eggs, you can make them in various combinations and eat them every day.

INGREDIENTS: eggs, spices

TUNA SPAGHETTI

If you don't like fish, you can try it with pasta and it will taste much better. If you're eating canned fish, make sure you get rid of the oil.

INGREDIENTS: tuna, spaghetti.

SARDINE TOAST

Similar to the meal above, eating fish in a different way can change the taste. I sometimes put sardines on a toast with cottage cheese. If you're eating canned sardines, make sure you get rid of the oil.

INGREDIENTS: sardines, toast, cottage cheese.

SEMOLINA WITH WHEY

A meal that I used to eat when I was a kid. I just replaced cocoa with whey protein. Make sure you don't add any sugar.

INGREDIENTS: semolina, whey, milk
