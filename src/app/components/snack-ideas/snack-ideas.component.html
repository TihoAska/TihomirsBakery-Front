snack ideas.

these are my healty snack ideas. they always replace my unhealthy snacks full of salt, sugar and empty calories.

HAM SANDWICH

A quick meal that you can pack and eat at scool or an office. Pay attention to the ingredients, make sure they are of good quality.

INGREDIENTS: whole grain bread, ham, cheese, salad.

BANANA RICE CAKES

A great meal to have instead of your usual sweets. Rice cakes are a great choice because they are 100% rice. Make sure the peanut butter is of a good quality.

INGREDIENTS: rice cake, peanut butter, banana, cocoa/cinnamon.

FRUIT SALAD

Great meal to get your nutrients and vitamins in. You can pick any fruit you want, but I always put banana, kiwi and an apple. Pour some honey over it if it's too sour for you.

INGREDIENTS: fruit of your choice.

WHEY WITH FRUIT

If you're in a hurry, just mix some whey protein with water and grab any fruit you like. You'll get the protein and nutrients for the morning, but you will be hungry soon, so make sure you pack yourself one of the meals mentioned before.

INGREDIENTS: whey, fruit.
