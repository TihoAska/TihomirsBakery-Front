lunch ideas.

lunch ideas that can be made in various combinations. my main ingredients are always some kind of meat, healthy carbs and some kind of vegetables.

CHICKEN WITH PASTA

One of my favourite meals that I could eat every day. Chicken should be fried on butter, cook pasta and broccoli together, stir it together with creme and put some cheese on top.

INGREDIENTS: chicken, pasta, broccoli, cheese, creme.

BEEF WITH TOMATO SAUCE

Beef is the best type of meat for building muscles, but it contains more fat and cholesterole than chicken so don't eat it too often.

INGREDIENTS: ground beef, tomato sauce, pasta, parsley.

CHICKEN WITH RICE AND BROCCOLI

Similar meal to the meal above, you can replace pasta with rice. Tomato sauce goes better with this combination.

INGREDIENTS: chicken, rice, broccoli, tomato sauce.

CHICKEN TORTILLA

When I feel like adding more vegetables or toppings to my meal, I simply wrap my meal in a tortilla. I usually add some corn and pickles. Try to find a good sauce that goes with it.

INGREDIENTS: wheat tortilla, chicken, corn, salad, tomato sauce.
