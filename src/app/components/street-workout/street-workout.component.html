STREET WORKOUT

STREET WORKOUT IS A WORKOUT STYLE IN A PARK OR ON A STREET, WHEREVER YOU CAN FIND BARS.


EXERCISES ARE DONE WITH YOUR OWN BODY AND RANGE FROM VERY EASY ONES THAT EVERYONE CAN DO TO VERY HARD ONES THAT ONLY EXPERIENCED ATHLETES CAN DO AND BEGINNERS CAN ONLY ADMIRE.


HERE I WILL SHOW YOU SOME EXERCISES THAT ARE ENOUGH TO BUILD MUSCLES AND A FINE BODY.


EVEN THOUGH YOU CAN BUILD A NICE BODY WITH THESE EXERCISES, THERE ARE SOME MUSCLE GROUPS THAT WILL FALL BEHIND, BECAUSE YOU CANNOT TARGET EVERY SINGLE MUSCLE WITH THIS STYLE OF WORKOUT.


FOR BUILDING A MORE COMPLETE AND ALL ROUND BODY YOU SHOULD CHECK OUT THE GYM PAGE.

PULLUPS

Great exercise for building upper body strength that targets back and arm muscles. This is a MUST DO exercise.

Palms are facing away from you, retract your back, tighten your core and legs and pull yourself up.

MUSCLES: Back, Biceps, Forearms

DIFFICULTY: Moderate

MUSCLE UP

A show-off exercise. Doesn't really build muscle or require strength. It takes time to learn the technique.

Get into pullup position, swing yourself forward, pull yourself up with back muscles, swing your legs and lean forward at the top.

MUSCLES: Back, Triceps

DIFFICULTY: Hard
